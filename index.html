Our Mission

Welcome to The Second Chapter. This platform is a personal initiative to share my carefully curated collection of books, which I have read and preserved. I believe that every book deserves a second life and a new home where it can continue to inspire. All books are offered at very affordable prices, ranging from 1.5 to 4.5 KWD. We offer free pickup at Kuwait University campuses or home delivery across Kuwait to make your reading journey easier.
