Our Mission

Reading is not just a hobby; it is a parallel life lived between pages and a free ticket to travel through time and minds. The Second Chapter was born from a deep belief that knowledge is a living entity that thrives when passed from one hand to another. Our vision is to transform personal books that have fulfilled their mission into new beacons of inspiration for others. We aim to build a sustainable reading community in Kuwait, offering curated literature at affordable prices (1.5 - 4 KWD). By providing flexible delivery and free pickup at Kuwait University campuses, we ensure that every student and book lover has access to the wisdom they seek.
